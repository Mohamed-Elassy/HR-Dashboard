## üìñ The Story
In this project, I developed a comprehensive HR Analytics Dashboard to support strategic workforce decision-making.  
The dashboard provides HR managers and leadership with a complete view of employee demographics, satisfaction, promotion readiness, and retention trends. By visualizing these metrics across two interactive pages, the organization can proactively manage talent, reduce attrition risks, and plan career development initiatives.

## üí° Key Insights
- **Departmental Structure:** Research & Development represents the largest department, followed by Sales and Human Resources.
- **Promotion Pipeline:** Only a small portion of employees are due for promotion, suggesting the need to review career progression paths.
- **Job Satisfaction:** A majority of employees report high satisfaction, reflecting a generally positive work environment.
- **Workload Indicator:** The high overtime rate may indicate productivity demands and should be monitored to reduce burnout risk.
- **Retention Strength:** A 92% on-service rate highlights strong workforce stability.
